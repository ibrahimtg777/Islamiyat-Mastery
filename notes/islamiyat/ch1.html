Islamiyat Notes

Chapter 1: The Life of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) in Makkah

This chapter covers the early life of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) in Makkah, including his childhood, early trade work, and the first revelation in Cave Hira.

Chapter 2: The Hijrah to Madinah

This chapter discusses the migration of Muslims from Makkah to Madinah, the reasons for migration, and its importance in Islamic history.

Chapter 3: The Constitution of Madinah

This chapter explains the first written agreement in Islamic history, which established peace among Muslims, Jews, and other groups in Madinah.

Chapter 4: The Battles of Badr, Uhud, and Khandaq

This chapter covers the key battles fought by the Muslims during the Prophet's (PBUH) time, their causes, outcomes, and lessons learned.

Chapter 5: The Treaty of Hudaybiyyah

This chapter explains the peace agreement between the Muslims and Quraysh, its terms, and how it became a victory for Islam despite initial concerns.

Chapter 6: The Conquest of Makkah

This chapter discusses the events leading to the peaceful conquest of Makkah and the Prophet's (PBUH) mercy towards his former enemies.

Chapter 7: The Farewell Pilgrimage

This chapter describes the Prophet's (PBUH) last pilgrimage, the important sermon delivered, and the key Islamic teachings emphasized.

Chapter 8: The Death of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

This chapter discusses the final illness of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), his passing, and the immediate events after his death.
